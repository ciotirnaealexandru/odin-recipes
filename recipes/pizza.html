Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C) and roll out pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and bell peppers.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
